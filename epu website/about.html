About Ethiopian Police University

Excellence in Law Enforcement Education Since Establishment

Our History

The Ethiopian Police University was established to meet the growing need for professional, well-educated law enforcement officers in Ethiopia. Our institution has evolved from a training center into a comprehensive university offering degree programs in various aspects of police science and criminal justice.

Over the years, we have graduated thousands of officers who serve with distinction across the nation, upholding the law and protecting citizens with integrity and professionalism.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education and training in law enforcement, developing ethical, competent, and professional police officers who serve the Ethiopian people with dedication, integrity, and respect for human rights.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading institution for police education in Africa, setting standards for excellence in law enforcement training and contributing to a safer, more just society.

Core Values

  • Integrity & Ethics
  • Professional Excellence
  • Service to Community
  • Respect for Human Rights
  • Continuous Learning
