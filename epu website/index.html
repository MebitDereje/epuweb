About Ethiopian Police University

The Ethiopian Police University stands as a beacon of excellence in law enforcement education, dedicated to developing highly skilled, ethical, and professional police officers who serve with integrity and commitment to justice.

Our institution combines rigorous academic training with practical field experience, preparing graduates to meet the evolving challenges of modern policing in Ethiopia.
